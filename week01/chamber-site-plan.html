Natal Chamber Of Commerce

Site Description

The Natal Chamber of Commerce acts to promote and support the growth of businesses, fostering unity among business owners. Among the services we offer are resources and education to assist business owners, legal support for businesses, and problem-solving to maintain ethics and benefit the entire community.

Scenarios

  1. Who are the leaders or members of the Chamber of Commerce's board, and what is the process for electing or appointing these leaders?
  2. How can I get in touch with the Chamber of Commerce for additional information or to participate in their events and programs?
  3. In the past year, what positive impacts has the Chamber of Commerce helped achieve?
